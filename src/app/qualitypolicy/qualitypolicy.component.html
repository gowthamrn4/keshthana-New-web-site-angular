Quality Policy

We at Keshthana consistently deliver services, meeting our customer satisfaction in-time, first time and every time through innovation and continual improvement.



Quality Objectives

  • Work towards our motto: "Quality at all costs" and inculcating a quality culture among employees.

  • Provide necessary resources to achieve required quality standards.
  • Ensure Quality /Workmanship Quality Education System (QES/WQES) training programs to employees at all levels.
  • Periodic monitoring of quality standards, Analyze Failures and take Corrective actions.

  • Record client feedback on quality levels delivered.
