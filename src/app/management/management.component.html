V.Radhakrishnan, a Civil Engineer, is the founder and Managing Director of Keshthana Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd..He incorporated the company in April 2012.

He is a first generation Entrepreneur, acquiring over 25 years of extensive background and affluent experience in various sectors of construction industry.

His experiences include Construction of Heavy Industrial Buildings, High Raised Residential, Institutional and Commercial Buildings, Express ways, Bridges, Marine infrastructures, Sewage Treatment Plants, Water Treatment Plants, Design and construction of IBS (Industrialized Building Systems) and Construction of vibration prevention walls and Sound barrier walls.

Prior to his entrepreneurship, he worked with leading Construction Companies in South East Asian countries (Australia, Singapore, Malaysia and Indonesia). He also associated with Ramky Infrastructure Ltd, Consolidated Construction Consortium Ltd and MARG Ltd., by heading the Business Units at Regional levels in India.

He is an eminent guest lecturer in Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College & Sri Ramanujar Engineering College, Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
